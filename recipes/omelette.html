Omelette

Back to Home

Description

An omelette is a dish made for humans using eggs and basic spices and salt.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • A cube of Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Haldi powder
  • Red Mirchi powder
Steps
  1. Break the eggs in a bowl and add salt, black pepper, haldi powder and red mirchi powder to it. Mix it well.
  2. Heat a pan and add butter to it. Once the butter melts, pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  3. Cook on medium heat until the edges start to lift from the pan. Then flip it and cook for another minute.
  4. Serve hot with bread or as is.
