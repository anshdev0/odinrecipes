Bhurji

Back to Home

Description

Bhurji is a dish made for humans using eggs and basic spices and salt. It is similar to an omelette but is scrambled and cooked with onions, tomatoes and other vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Haldi powder
  • Red Mirchi powder
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
Steps
  1. Chop the onion and tomato into small pieces.
  2. Heat a pan and add oil to it. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onions and tomatoes to it. Cook until the onions are translucent and the tomatoes are soft.
  3. Break the eggs in a bowl and add salt, black pepper, haldi powder and red mirchi powder to it. Mix it well.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan with the onions and tomatoes. Cook on medium heat, stirring continuously until the eggs are fully cooked and scrambled with the vegetables.
  5. Serve hot with bread or as is.
